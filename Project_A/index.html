The extraterrestrial life forms in the awakening of alien stars have super strong reproductive ability, spread outwards, and have extremely strong vitality. This creature is to some extent derived from extraterrestrial life in movies, but it is slightly microscopic, non aggressive, and suitable for existing in liquid environments.

Habitat:

Liquid

Appearance:

The biological core is spike shaped and circular, generating cells in random shapes of squares, triangles, and circles. There is a filamentous connection between the core and cells.

Key Characteristics:

  • Eternal Core
  • Partially Visible
  • Colorful

What we know so far about this creature: Randomly generated, non aggressive, and interactive.
